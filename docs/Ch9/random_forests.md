# 9.2 Random Forests for Patient Outcomes

Random Forests, a renowned ensemble method, leverages the power of decision trees but introduces randomness to enhance model diversity and, consequently, performance.

- By utilizing multiple decision trees and aggregating their predictions, random forests can offer superior insights into patient outcomes.

- One of the key strengths of random forests in a healthcare context is its ability to provide **feature importance metrics**. This allows clinicians to identify crucial health indicators, helping in understanding disease mechanisms and driving preventive strategies.
