# 12.3 Topic Modeling in Clinical Notes

The vast expanse of clinical notes holds numerous topics ranging from symptoms and treatments to patient histories and prognoses.

- **Extracting Key Topics**: Algorithms and methods to discern the principal themes present in a myriad of clinical documents.
- **Use Cases**: Discovering prevalent symptoms or treatments frequently mentioned in patient notes, leading to improved patient care strategies.
